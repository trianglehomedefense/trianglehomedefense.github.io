Home / Wildlife

Wildlife problems start with access.

We inspect the home, document visible evidence, and explain what conditions are allowing animal activity.

Inspection-first

Wildlife service without scare tactics.

Triangle Home Defense focuses on the cause of the issue: where the animal is entering, what the home is offering, and what needs to be corrected to reduce repeat activity. We evaluate accessible exterior areas, rooflines, vents, crawlspaces, attics, foundation transitions, and visible damage indicators.

Squirrels

Roofline gaps, soffit/fascia damage, chew points, attic indicators, and repeated entry risk.

Birds

Vent nesting, ledges, openings, and areas where screening or exclusion may be appropriate.

Snakes and rodents

Foundation gaps, crawlspace access, utility penetrations, debris pressure, and movement routes.

Scope note: Final recommendations depend on species, access, safety conditions, season, legal restrictions, and what is visible at the time of inspection.
